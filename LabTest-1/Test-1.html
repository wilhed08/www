My experience in Sault College

I am an international student and I chose this college for the following reasons:
  • The college is ranked as one of the best in Ontario.
  • Their computer programming diploma is the most comprehensive one available in Canada.
  • If you want to continue your studies at the university, this college offers the best education in the city.
  • The teachers who teach this diploma are more experienced in the field and their patience is amazing.
  • On campus you can do different activities such as bonfires, board games, get to know different cultures, among others.
  • & Remember that it is located in the beautiful city of Sault Ste. Marie.
